## QR Code Placement

- Position: Bottom-right corner of last page
- Size: 60x60 pixels
- Contains: `{BASE_URL}/docs/validate?id={document_id}&checksum={first_8_chars}`
- Label below QR: "Scan to verify authenticity"

## Trade-offs

| Decision | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| QR in footer vs watermark | Footer is less intrusive and doesn't obscure content |
| Recalculate checksum after sign | Ensures validated checksum matches the signed file |
| pdf-lib for manipulation | Lightweight, browser-compatible, no external dependencies |
